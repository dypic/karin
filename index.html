The Clinic may operate "cookies," which are files that store and retrieve information about you as needed. A cookie is a string of information sent by a web server to a web browser, stored temporarily, and sent back to the server upon subsequent requests. When you access the website, the Clinic can read the contents of cookies stored in your browser, enabling services to be provided without requiring additional information such as your name.

The information collected through cookies is limited to the member's ID and does not include other personal information. The ID collected through cookies may be used for the following purposes:

- To provide differentiated screen information based on individual interests
- To track user navigation for personalized services during subsequent visits

You have the option to accept or decline the installation of cookies. By accessing "Tools > Internet Options > Privacy > Advanced" on your web browser, you can choose to accept all cookies, be notified when cookies are set, or reject all cookies. Please note that rejecting cookies may lead to inconvenience in service use or difficulty in providing services.

Cookies expire upon browser closure or logout.
