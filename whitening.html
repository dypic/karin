KARIN DERMATOLOGY

Whitening Treatment Devices

PicoPlus

The 450-picosecond laser (measured in trillionths of a second) precisely targets lesions, breaking down pigment particles into smaller fragments for faster and more effective treatment.

Clarity

Clarity is a versatile device that allows the selection of specific wavelengths to address various skin concerns, such as pigmentation, hair removal, and vascular issues. Equipped with an automatic cooling system, it ensures treatments are performed with minimal discomfort.

Potenza

Potenza delivers RF (radiofrequency) energy through micro-needles inserted into the dermis layer, minimizing surface damage while promoting collagen regeneration. This process improves skin texture and enhances overall skin quality.

CO2

This treatment works on the principle of laser absorption by the water naturally present in our tissues, instantly vaporizing it. This mechanism enables the effective treatment of various skin conditions.

Skin Booster

A treatment that injects skin-regenerating components directly into the skin to address underlying issues and restore its natural health.

LDM

A high-density ultrasound energy device that boosts the skin's immune system by up to 1800%. It draws moisture into the dermis, promoting healthier and more elastic skin.

O2 Derm

High concentrations of oxygen and negative ions suppress free radicals that accelerate skin aging, support skin cell regeneration, protect the skin barrier, and maintain long-lasting hydration.
