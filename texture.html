KARIN DERMATOLOGY

Texture Treatment Devices

Potenza

Potenza delivers RF (radiofrequency) energy through micro-needles inserted into the dermis layer, minimizing surface damage while promoting collagen regeneration. This process improves skin texture and enhances overall skin quality.

Clarity

Clarity is a versatile device that allows the selection of specific wavelengths to address various skin concerns, such as pigmentation, hair removal, and vascular issues. Equipped with an automatic cooling system, it ensures treatments are performed with minimal discomfort.

CO2

This treatment works on the principle of laser absorption by the water naturally present in our tissues, instantly vaporizing it. This mechanism enables the effective treatment of various skin conditions.

Pico Fraxel

The 450-picosecond laser (measured in trillionths of a second) precisely targets lesions, breaking down pigment particles into smaller fragments, enabling faster and more effective treatment.

Exosome

A skin booster crafted from the essence of stem cell core components. It utilizes highly purified and refined stem cell culture fluid to effectively repair damaged skin and revitalize the skin's natural energy.

Karin Scar Injection

Micro-needles are inserted into the dermis layer to deliver RF (radiofrequency) energy, minimizing surface damage while stimulating collagen regeneration to improve skin texture.

JuveLook

JuveLook is a self-regenerating collagen booster that combines high-molecular-weight PLA with hyaluronic acid, both naturally present in human skin tissues. It stimulates tissue regeneration and enhances skin elasticity and hydration.

Rejuran

Rejuran is a skin rejuvenation treatment containing polynucleotide (PN), a growth factor complex. It regenerates and revitalizes the epidermal and dermal layers, enhancing skin health, elasticity, and overall firmness.

LDM

A high-density ultrasound energy device that boosts the skin's immune system by up to 1800%. It draws moisture into the dermis, promoting healthier and more elastic skin.

Belotero Filler

Belotero Filler is an FDA-approved Swiss hyaluronic acid filler known for its safety and natural results. It offers four product lines designed for temporary facial wrinkle reduction and volume restoration.

Aqua Peel

A procedure that effectively removes Demodex mites, impurities, sebum, and dead skin cells from pores using AHA and BHA peeling solutions while maintaining skin hydration.
