KARIN DERMATOLOGY

Anti-Aging Treatment Devices

Ulthera

Focused ultrasound lifting that powerfully targets the fascia layer contracts the sagging SMAS layer, improving wrinkles and sagging skin. Collagen regeneration ensures long-lasting lifting effects.

Shurink

Ultrasound lifting that focuses energy on the SMAS layer with minimal damage to the skin's surface. It tightens sagging tissues, delivering noticeable lifting effects.

Potenza

Potenza delivers RF (radiofrequency) energy through micro-needles inserted into the dermis layer, minimizing surface damage while promoting collagen regeneration. This process improves skin texture and enhances overall skin quality.

Oligio

Oligio is a non-invasive non-invasive RF lifting treatment that delivers RF energy deep into the dermis, strengthening the skin from within. It helps improve skin condition by stimulating collagen production and enhancing elasticity.

Clarity

Clarity is a versatile device that allows the selection of specific wavelengths to address various skin concerns, such as pigmentation, hair removal, and vascular issues. Equipped with an automatic cooling system, it ensures treatments are performed with minimal discomfort.

LDM

A high-density ultrasound energy device that boosts the skin's immune system by up to 1800%. It draws moisture into the dermis, promoting healthier and more elastic skin.

JuveLook

JuveLook is a self-regenerating collagen booster that combines high-molecular-weight PLA with hyaluronic acid, both naturally present in human skin tissues. It stimulates tissue regeneration and enhances skin elasticity and hydration.

Rejuran

Rejuran is a skin rejuvenation treatment containing polynucleotide (PN), a growth factor complex. It regenerates and revitalizes the epidermal and dermal layers, enhancing skin health, elasticity, and overall firmness.
